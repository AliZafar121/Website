Doctor

A doctor is a medical professional trained to diagnose, treat, and prevent illnesses and injuries. Doctors play a critical role in healthcare, working to improve the health and well-being of individuals and communities.

key Responsibility

  • Diagnosis:
    Identifying medical conditions through patient history, physical examinations, and diagnostic tests.
  • Treatment:
    Prescribing medications, performing surgeries, or providing therapies tailored to patients' needs.
  • Prevention:
    Promoting healthy lifestyles and administering vaccinations to prevent diseases.
  • Education:
    Guiding patients on health management and offering advice on maintaining overall wellness.
