Teacher

A teacher is an educator who imparts knowledge, skills, and values to students. Teachers play a pivotal role in shaping minds and fostering personal and academic growth.

Key Responsibility

  • Instructions
    Delivering lessons on various subjects tailored to students' levels.
  • Guidance
    Supporting students' intellectual and emotional development.
  • Assessment
    Evaluating student progress through tests, assignments, and observations.
  • Preparation
    Planning lessons, activities, and teaching materials.
  • Mentorship
    Encouraging curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
